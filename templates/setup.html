Setup

Follow the instructions below to setup and configure your SMARS Robot.

Step 1 - Check wiring


Plug in each servo to the PCA9685 driver board. The correct wiring sequence is as follows:

Channel 0LEFT_LEG_FRONT
Channel 1LEFT_FOOT_FRONT
Channel 2LEFT_LEG_BACK
Channel 3LEFT_FOOT_BACK
Channel 4RIGHT_LEG_BACK
Channel 5RIGHT_FOOT_BACK
Channel 6RIGHT_LEG_FRONT
Channel 7RIGHT_FOOT_FRONT

Press the round button below to test each servo is responding (it will wiggle when pressed).
